How to Measure

Foot Length Stand on a sheet of paper. Mark the back of your heel and the tip of your longest toe. Measure the distance — the longer foot sets your size.
Note: Sizing can vary slightly by manufacturer. Check individual product descriptions for specific fit details, and when in doubt, size up for a roomier fit.
