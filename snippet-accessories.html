How to Measure

Head Wrap the tape just above your ears and eyebrows, level around the back of your head — the tape should sit where a hat brim naturally rests.
Hand Measure around your dominant palm at the widest point, just below the knuckles, excluding the thumb.
Sock Size Match your US shoe size to the size range in the sock chart above.
Note: Sizing can vary slightly by manufacturer. Check individual product descriptions for specific fit details, and when in doubt, size up for a roomier fit.
