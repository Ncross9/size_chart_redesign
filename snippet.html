How to Measure

Chest / Bust Measure under your arms and around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape straight across your back.
Waist Measure around your natural waistline (the smallest part), snug but comfortable.
Hips Stand with heels together and measure around the fullest part of your hips.
Sleeve Bend your elbow 90°. Measure from the center back neck, over the shoulder, to the elbow, and down to the wrist.
Inseam Measure from the interior leg seam (crotch) to the bottom of your ankle bone.
Note: Sizing can vary slightly by manufacturer. Check individual product descriptions for specific fit details, and when in doubt, size up for a roomier fit.
